Favorite Applications

    Messenger



    One of my favorite applications is messenger, because this is a very useful app. It helps me to
    communicate with other people. It has a feature where you can create a group chat, which enables
    a better communication between the group. You can also send pictures, document files, and videos.
    With that it can help me express my feelings better through sending of memes/pictures. I also
    put some of my school work files in here, especially the codes, research papers and assignments
    in here. The app also has a videocall feature which helps me feel like I'm just close to the
    person/people talking to. You can also send a message for free because of free data though this
    limits its feature. That last feature is pretty useful for me because I don’t usually buy load
    for my internet use, thats why this is a very helpful app for me.


    Facebook


    Things that I liked in this application is that I can be updated to the news for whatever is
    happening to the world or to my friends or relatives. It helped me learned a lot about people’s
    likes and dislikes, though there are a lot of fake news, it makes you really want to research
    thoroughly every post you see. I use this app to pass time by scrolling the news feed searching
    for memes and sharing it with other people. It helps me express my feelings creatively and is my leisure app.



    Steam


    I like this application because this serves as a platform for games, messaging, trading, and buying items
    which is all game related. Steam has also a lot of games to offer. It also lets you interact with
    strangers you meet on the games in steam, which gives you a perspective on what are the characteristics
    of other players in the world. If steam helps me interact with strangers, it lets me interact better
    with my friends whenever we play a game. It also has a steam guard which secures your account.
