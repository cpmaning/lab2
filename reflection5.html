Reflection 5


SOCIT Week


    This year s SOCIT fest was kind of a downer since there s not much activity to do. It s just a
    bunch of seminars, but quite educational, so I guess its ok. The speakers in the seminars shared their
    experiences on how they started from the bottom until to what they are right now. Since some of the
    speakers are the CEO s of their own company. You can really see that the job they do is really their
    passion, you can see in the way they speak the energy and how excited they are to share their
    experiences in life and explaining to us about the gaming industry here in the Philippines. It really
    surprised me on how old the gaming industry here in the Philippines. They said that it s already 15 years
    old here, I thought that the industry was at least 10 years old or something. That kind of made me feel old.


    I still hoped that the SOCIT fest would have more activities for the students to do, like more
    tournaments like DOTA 2 or LOL. Since the tournaments that week was only just mobile legends and
    Tekken. We couldve joined the mobile legends tournament, but we have a quiz bee on our subject
    making us unable to join. Overall it was an ok fest, so Im hoping for a better fest next year.
